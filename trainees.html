Trainee Opportunities

Start your journey with Refuel Haydock — learn new skills, gain confidence, and be part of a friendly community café.

Learn, Grow & Get Involved

At Refuel Haydock, we believe in giving local people the chance to build real experience in a supportive environment. Whether you're a student, looking for work experience, or wanting to learn new skills, our trainee roles help you gain confidence while contributing to the community.

Our Trainee Programmes

Barista Trainee

Learn how to prepare coffees, teas, and speciality drinks using our commercial machines. You’ll be trained by our experienced team and supported every step of the way.

6–12 week programme

Kitchen Assistant Trainee

Get hands‑on experience preparing breakfasts, brunch dishes, and our popular loaded fries. Learn safe food handling, plating, and kitchen organisation.

4–10 week programme

Community Events Trainee

Help run our Warm Wednesdays, kids’ craft sessions, and seasonal events. Perfect for anyone who enjoys working with people and supporting the local community.

Flexible weekly sessions

What You'll Learn

  • Customer service and communication skills
  • Food hygiene and safe kitchen practices
  • How to use barista equipment
  • Teamwork in a fast‑paced café environment
  • Confidence working with the public
  • Event support and community engagement

Who Can Apply?

Our trainee roles are open to anyone aged 16+ who wants to gain experience in hospitality or community work. No previous experience is needed — just a positive attitude and willingness to learn.
