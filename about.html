Community & Events

REFUEL is a neighbourhood hub at 284 Church Road — we serve great food, but what really defines us is the way we bring people together. From local meet‑ups to seasonal specials, our calendar is built around community.

Regular Activities

  • Run Club: community 5K social runs that finish back at REFUEL for coffee and conversation.
  • Seniors Afternoon Tea: warm, friendly gatherings with subsidised tea & cake.
  • Monday Crafts & Kids Workshops: family‑friendly sessions during school holidays.
  • Warm Wednesdays: affordable soup and roll lunches to support local neighbours.

Seasonal Events & Specials

We celebrate seasonal occasions with themed menus and events — Valentines specials, Christmas menus, bottomless brunches, steak nights, live‑music evenings and street‑food drops. Follow our socials or call to check upcoming dates.

Private Bookings & Community Hire

REFUEL hosts private parties, local group meetups and community fundraisers. Get in touch to discuss bespoke menus, group packages and event dates.
